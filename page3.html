TOP TWO BOWLERS OF ALL TIME





DALE STEYN


Dale Willem Steyn born 27 June 1983 is a South African former professional cricketer who played for the South African Cricket Team.
He is often regarded as one of the greatest fast bowlers of all time and the best Test bowler of his generation.Fans will remember his
inspiring performance against India in the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, where he stole the show with a match-winning 5-55 against India.
Steyn is one of the world's greatest quicks, taking more that 400 Test wickets at the sublime average of 22.8.











SHOAIB AKHTAR


Shoaib Akhtar born on 13 August 1975) is a Pakistani former cricketer and commentator. Nicknamed the "Rawalpindi Express", he was the first bowler to be recorded bowling at 100 miles per hour, a feat he achieved twice in his career.He was an amalgamation of raw pace, lethal bouncers and nightmares. There was hardly anyone who got away with it.
