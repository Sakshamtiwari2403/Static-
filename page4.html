TOP TWO ALLROUNDER OF ALL TIME







KAPIL DEV


Kapil Dev, in full Kapil Dev Ramlal Nikhanj, (born January 6, 1959, Chandigarh, India), Indian cricketer and the greatest
pace bowler in his country's history. He is the only cricketer to have scored over 5,000 runs and taken more than 400 wickets
in Test (international match) cricket.







JACQUES KALLIS


Jacques Henry Kallis (born 16 October 1975) is a South African cricket coach and former cricketer. Widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time and as South Africa's greatest batsman ever, he is a right-handed batsman and right-arm fast-medium swing bowler.
